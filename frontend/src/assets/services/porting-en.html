Application porting means changing, transfer and adaptation of a software app for use in a different environment. Despite the transformation, the previous functionality of the program remains unaffected while some additional benefits can be achieved due to the options of a new platform.

The reasons for porting apps and libraries may be the need to expand the capabilities of the software solution, improve its market position, and increase the competitiveness of the application.

We offer services for porting libraries and mobile apps to the platform you need.

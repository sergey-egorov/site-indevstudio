The Raspberry Pi is an amazing little computer that can be used for many tasks. You can browse the internet on it, play games, and even watch HD videos.

Among the examples of uses of Raspberry Pi are security cameras, digital photo frames, smart tv, network monitoring tool, controlling robots, home automation systems. It proved to be very useful in providing the functions of IoT devices. Raspberry Pi based IoT projects are very popular due to their low cost, affordability and ease of implementation.

On-board system apps make your vehicle more connected and smarter. Such an app is able to provide connection to your machine, its systems and sensors and get analytics to the workflow.

The example of such an app can be the one designed for interaction with truck drivers during their logistical activities which includes such components and features as speech recognition, speech synthesis, face detection, data recording, communication system and others.

We offer app development for on-board computers to monitor your fleet and drivers, help digitalise and ease the workflow, vehicle data retrieving and sharing information between the board, office and partners.

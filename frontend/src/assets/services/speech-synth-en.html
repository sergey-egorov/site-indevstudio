Speech synthesizers are special programs consisting of a certain number of modules that provide the ability to translate typed texts into voice that sound naturally human.

Speech synthesizers no longer seem as unusual as they used to be. Text-to-speech software is becoming more commonly used, allowing us to engage in other activities at the same time, whether it be walking, driving, household chores, or similar.

Text-to-speech software is also popular in business as it gives voices to services making them more personal and direct.

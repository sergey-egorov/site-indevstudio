Speech recognition systems are changing the way people interact with their devices. They allow us to talk to a device and get a response to our question or command.

Speech recognition technologies are used especially for digital assistants designed to help us perform different tasks or get necessary information in real time. Speech recognition technologies find applications in many industries and situations: in our daily activities, in the workplace, in banking, marketing, healthcare, with the Internet of things, in language learning.

The main idea of the Internet of Things (IoT) is to equip objects with a technology that allows them to communicate with each other without human participation.

IoT can make our lives easier by turning physical objects into active participants in business processes and helpers in our daily life routine.

It is possible to control IoT devices from a distance and exchange data in real time without human-to-human or human-to-computer interaction and, thus, to perform work and household chores more quickly and efficiently.

IoT finds many applications in both services sector and industrial settings. Regardless of the goal, the implementation of a particular IoT system is associated with the following tasks: device management, data collection and analysis, and deployment of mobile cloud services.

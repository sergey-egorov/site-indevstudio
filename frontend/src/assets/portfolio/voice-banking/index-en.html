Problems to solve

In preparation for the presentation of a new product from our partner, Speech Technology Center, we were offered to develop a demo application for mobile devices that could demonstrate the possibilities of using voice biometrics as an on-board technology for mobile devices.

Solutions

To show the potential of our partner’s new voice recognition technology, we chose to create a banking app. To start using the app, a user must pass the registration procedure that includes creating a voice model. After that, the functions of conducting banking transactions initiated by the voice become available. For example, to make a money transfer, the only thing a user needs to do is to voice who and what sum he or she wants to remit. The transaction is completed only in case of successful user’s voice identification.
